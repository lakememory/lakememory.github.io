About Me

I'm James Hinton, an electronic music artist (performing as The Range) and creative technologist with a background in physics. My work explores the intersection of music, technology, and human connection.

With a career spanning over a decade in electronic music production, I've released four albums, including two on Domino Recordings, toured globally, and accumulated over 10 million streams across platforms.

Alongside my music career, I've worked in diverse fields including value investing as a Co-CIO at Young Orchard and co-founded Glover Hinton, a creative agency specializing in multidisciplinary projects. I'm currently developing Auteur, an LLM-based generative music application for municipalities.

My approach to creativity involves recombining existing ideas in unexpected ways and immersing myself in unfamiliar contexts to gain new perspectives. I'm passionate about creating experiences that help people understand complex systems and express themselves in new ways.
