Engineering

As Partner & Co-founder of Glover Hinton, I specialize in multidisciplinary projects requiring rapid execution across music, design, and technology. We work with clients who need nimble teams that can navigate complex problems where larger teams would be inefficient.

Currently, we are developing Auteur, an LLM-based generative ambient music application for municipalities. This project combines my technical development skills in Python and machine learning with my musical composition to create generative music experiences with integrated reporting capabilities.

Music

As The Range, I create electronic music that explores the intersection of technology and human connection. I've released four albums, including two on Domino Recordings ("Potential" in 2016 and "Mercury" in 2022), toured globally, and accumulated over 25 million streams across platforms.

What makes my work distinctive, I think, is my sampling approach. For "Potential," I combed through hundreds of hours of YouTube videos to find unknown artists with fewer than 1,000 views, whose voices resonated with something authentic and unpolished. This process evolved from simply preferring how these raw vocals worked with my synthesizers into something much more meaningful - a way to showcase incredible talent that algorithms might never surface. In order to showcase the people on the album we made "Superimpose," a documentary where we actually met these artists and explored their stories.

Otherwise

My background spans diverse fields, including a Bachelor's degree in Physics from Brown University (2010) and experience as Co-Chief Investment Officer at Young Orchard (2018-2023), where I helped achieve 136% returns over a five-year period through value investing strategies. My approach to creativity involves recombining existing ideas in unexpected ways and I'm passionate about creating experiences that help people understand complex systems through my work.
